Donations Overview

The AMS enables donations to be processed as part of an organization's day-to-day business.  Donation Items can be created in Salesforce (as part of the standard Item creation process) and then organized into Catalogs for display in a related Store.  In turn, Donation Items can be "sold" and processed on both the standard Salesforce backend and through the Community eStore.  The eStore can be configured to accept both one-time and recurring donations in either stepped or custom amounts.
