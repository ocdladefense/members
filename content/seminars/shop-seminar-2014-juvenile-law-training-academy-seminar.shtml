2014 Juvenile Law Training Academy

Outside the Box: Practical Strategies for Juvenile Practitioners in Increasingly Complicated Cases

October 20–21, 2014
Eugene Hilton

Online registration is now closed. Please register ($135 lawyers, $110 nonlawyer CASA and nonlawyer CRB) at the door on Monday morning, October 20 at the Eugene Hilton.

Academy sponsors include the Juvenile Court Improvement Program, the Juvenile Law Section of the Oregon State Bar, the Oregon Criminal Defense Lawyers Association, the Office of Public Defense Services, the University of Oregon School of Law, and Youth, Rights & Justice, Attorneys at Law, plus the invaluable assistance of the Department of Justice and the CASA program. This seminar is for all juvenile court lawyers, including both state’s attorneys and attorneys for children and parents, and for Guardians Ad Litem and CASAs.
