Sentencing for Sophomores

Friday, March 27, 2015
OCDLA Office • 12:30 – 3:30 p.m. (or via GoToMeeting)

Registration: $49 by March 17 ($59 after March 17)

REGISTER to ATTEND LIVE at OCDLA office.

REGISTER to ATTEND ONLINE via GoToMeeting.

Presented by:

Jesse Wm. Barton, Attorney at Law, Salem

Sentencing for Sophomores” is an interactive and regularly updated Power-Point-based presentation designed to ground attorneys and legal professionals on the basics of Oregon felony sentencing law, and to introduce them to various advanced topics. It is designed to work in conjunction with the bi-annually Sentencing Outline found in Felony Sentencing in Oregon: Guidelines, Statutes, Cases (OCDLA 3d ed. 2012).
