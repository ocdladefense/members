Ken Morrow Lifetime Achievement Award

2014 Recipient Steve Wax

December 5, 2014 • 6:00 p.m. • The Benson Hotel, Portland

Please join the OCDLA Board of Directors in honoring Steve with the prestigious Ken Morrow Lifetime Achievement Award.

Master of Ceremonies: OCDLA President Eve Oldenkamp

The evening includes:
• Cocktail Reception
• Dinner
• Oral Auction
• Tribute with speakers Justice Richard Baldwin, Kathleen Haley, Tom Hillier, and Emily Simon.

Steve Wax will be the 11th recipient of the Ken Morrow Lifetime Achievement Award, which dates back to 2000. The award was created by OCDLA following the passing of renowned Eugene attorney Ken Morrow to recognize the lifelong commitment and significant achivements of attorneys who have worked in the defense community and those who have made important contributions to the administration of justice.
