Continuing Legal Education Seminars

OCDLA sponsors over a dozen continuing legal education seminars per year. Topics include DUII, search and seizure, trial preparation, jury selection, juvenile law, the death penalty, evidence, sentencing and more. All seminars are eligible for CLE credit from the Oregon State Bar as well as other bar organizations.
