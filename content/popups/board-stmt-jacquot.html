Megan Jacquot

Board Candidate Statement
District 3

I write to ask each for your vote in the upcoming board elections. I am running for my fourth term as a board member. I’ve served two two-year terms in the past, and am currently serving a two-year term.
In my time on the board I have been involved in some major decisions which will help our great organization remain at the forefront of organizations of its kind for many years. It is important to have a mix of new people with new ideas and more experienced board members with historical knowledge. During my time on the board I have been adept at helping new ideas take root and flourish. As I think about the sentence I just wrote, it seems like b. s. but I think others would say it’s true.

We still have work to do to solidify OCDLA’s position of leadership in the state and country, and I ask for your support.
