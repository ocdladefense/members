Olcott Thompson

Board Candidate Statement
District 6

I am interested in being a member of the OCDLA Board because I believe I can assist OCDLA in that capacity. I have been a member of the Board of Directors of the Marion County Association of Defenders (MCAD) for over 10 years. I was the President of the Board for two years and have been the MCAD Treasurer for the past three plus years. As an MCAD Board member I learned about the funding issues we and OPDS face.

I have been practicing criminal defense work primarily in Marion County since I was admitted to the bar in 1982. The best events I have attended have been connected with OCDLA and I want to make sure this organization continues its excellent events and continues to support its newer members. I also think we need to think about what we are providing our more experienced members, although the death penalty CLE every year should be attend by the “experienced” lawyers among us whether they are interested in doing death penalty work or not. The ideas and materials that come from that CLE are invaluable for non death penalty work.

I hope you will allow me to continue to serve the defense community in an area larger than just Marion County.
